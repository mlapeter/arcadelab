🧲Magnet Playground

🧲 Drag a magnet around. The glowing lines show its invisible magnetic field.

🔴 Red is the North pole, 🔵 blue is South. Opposite poles pull together, the same poles push apart.

🧭 The compass needle always turns to point along the field.

✨ The tiny iron filings line up with the field, just like real ones do!

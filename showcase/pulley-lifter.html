🏗️Pulley Lifter

How it works

A pulley is a wheel that changes the way you pull a rope.

With more pulleys sharing the load, you pull with less force — but you must pull more rope to lift the crate the same height.

2 pulleys = half the pull. 3 pulleys = a third of the pull. Machines trade effort for distance!

Drag the orange rope handle down to lift the crate

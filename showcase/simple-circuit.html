💡Simple Circuit

Electricity flows in a loop. When the switch is closed, current travels from the battery, through the wires, and lights the bulb.

Open the switch and the loop breaks — no current, no light.

Series puts two bulbs on one path, so they share the energy and look dimmer.

Parallel gives each bulb its own loop, so both shine full bright.

Tap the switch to turn the circuit on and off

🌊Wave Tank

Tap the water to make a ripple. It spreads out in a growing circle, just like a stone dropped in a pond.

Turn on the 2nd source and two sets of ripples will cross each other.

Where two crests meet, the wave gets extra big and bright — that is constructive interference.

Where a crest meets a dip, they cancel out and the water goes flat and dark.
